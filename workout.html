Workout Ideas

Quick and effective workouts for busy students.

Why Exercise?

Regular physical activity boosts energy, improves focus, reduces stress, and supports overall health. You don't need hours at the gym — just consistency!

10-Minute Workout Ideas

Squats

3 sets of 15 reps

Push-Ups

3 sets of 10 reps

Plank

Hold for 30-60 sec

Jumping Jacks

3 sets of 30 seconds

Cardio

Walk or jog 20-30 min

Tips for Success

  • Schedule workouts like you schedule classes.
  • Start small and build consistency.
  • Stay hydrated and listen to your body.
  • Find activities you enjoy.
